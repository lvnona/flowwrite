FlowWrite Mobile — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 May 2026

FlowWrite is a writing assistant for Android. It lets you dictate text by voice and rewrite or generate text using AI, then place that text into the app you're working in. This policy explains exactly what FlowWrite accesses, why, and what it never does.

Information we process

  • Account (email): When you sign in with Google, we store your email and a unique account ID to manage your plan and usage limits.
  • Voice audio: When you dictate, your audio is sent over an encrypted connection to a speech-to-text service for transcription and is immediately discarded. We do not keep recordings.
  • Text you generate: Text you type, dictate, or generate is sent over an encrypted connection to an AI service to produce your result, which is returned to you. We do not store this text on our servers.
  • Usage counts: We record counters (e.g. number of generations / audio words per week) to enforce plan limits.

How the Accessibility service is used

FlowWrite uses Android's Accessibility service for a single, specific purpose: to place your dictated or generated text into the text field you are actively writing in, and to read the text in that field when you ask FlowWrite to rewrite it.

  • It acts only on explicit user action (tapping the FlowWrite bubble or the Insert button).
  • It does not read your screen in the background, log your keystrokes, monitor other apps, or collect screen content.
  • Accessibility data is used solely on-device to insert/read the focused field and is never transmitted to us or sold.

Other permissions

  • Microphone — to capture your voice for dictation, only while you are recording.
  • Display over other apps — to show the floating FlowWrite bubble over the app you're using.
  • Notifications — to show the recording/processing status.

What we never do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not use your content to build advertising profiles.
  • We do not store your voice recordings or generated text on our servers.

Data sharing

To provide transcription and AI features, your audio and text are processed by third-party AI providers strictly to return your result. They are not permitted to use your content for their own purposes. Account data is stored in Google Firebase (authentication and database) under industry-standard security.

Data retention & deletion

Voice audio is discarded immediately after transcription. Account and usage records are kept while your account is active. To delete your account and associated data, contact us at the address below.

Children

FlowWrite is not directed to children under 13.
