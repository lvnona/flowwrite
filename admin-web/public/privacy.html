Privacy Policy

Effective date: 31 May 2026 · Last updated: 31 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information FlowWrite (“we”, “us”) collects when you use the FlowWrite desktop, mobile, and web applications (the “Service”), why we collect it, how we use and share it, and the choices you have. We aim to be honest and plain-spoken rather than legalistic. If anything here is unclear, write to us at flowwrite@u11.ca and we will explain.

1. Who we are

FlowWrite is operated by U11 (the “Operator”), based in Canada. We are the data controller for personal information collected through the Service.

2. Information we collect

Account information

When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, Google account ID, and profile photo URL. We do not receive your Google password. We store this information in our cloud database (Google Firestore) to identify your account.

Usage information

We keep counters of how often you use the Service — for example, how many AI generations and dictated words you have used this week, this month, and all-time. We use these counters to enforce free-plan limits and show you statistics on your dashboard.

Templates you create

Reusable writing templates you create are stored in your account so they sync between your devices.

Generation content (transient)

The text prompts you give FlowWrite — and the text it generates for you — are sent to an AI provider (see Section 4) to produce a response, then returned to your device. We do not, by default, retain a copy of the prompt or the response on our servers.

If you turn the optional “Save generation history” setting on inside the app, your generations are stored locally on your own device only. We never receive a copy. The setting is off by default.

Audio (transient)

When you use voice dictation, the audio your microphone captures is sent to a transcription provider (OpenAI Whisper), converted to text, and the text is returned to your device. We do not retain the audio. The transcription provider’s own data-handling policies apply (see Section 4).

Payment information

When you subscribe to a paid plan, payment is handled by Stripe. We never see, receive or store your card number, CVV, or bank details. Stripe sends us only a customer ID, subscription status, plan tier, and renewal date so we can unlock paid features.

App and device information

The Service may log standard diagnostic information such as the app version, operating system, and error reports. This helps us fix bugs.

3. How we use this information

  • To run the Service and deliver the features you ask for.
  • To enforce free-plan limits and unlock paid features for subscribers.
  • To respond to support requests you send us.
  • To send transactional emails (subscription confirmations, password-less account events, important service notices). We do not send marketing emails without your consent.
  • To detect and prevent abuse of the Service.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use it to train AI models. We do not show ads.

4. Third-party services we use

FlowWrite is built on top of a small number of trusted services. Each receives only what is needed to perform its function:

  • Google Firebase & Firestore — account, plan, usage counters, templates. (Firebase privacy)
  • Google Sign-In (OAuth) — authentication only. (Google privacy)
  • Anthropic (Claude API) — receives your prompt to generate text when Claude is the selected provider. Anthropic states it does not train models on API inputs by default. (Anthropic privacy)
  • OpenAI (ChatGPT & Whisper APIs) — receives your prompt or audio when OpenAI is the selected provider. OpenAI states it does not train models on API inputs by default. (OpenAI privacy)
  • DeepSeek — receives your prompt when DeepSeek is the selected provider. (DeepSeek)
  • Stripe — handles all payment processing. (Stripe privacy)
  • HostArmada — hosts flowwrite.u11.ca and supporting endpoints.
  • GitHub — hosts the download & auto-update files for the desktop app.

5. How we store and protect your data

  • Cloud data is stored in Google Firestore with encryption at rest and in transit.
  • API keys used by the Service are admin-managed server-side and never shipped to customer devices.
  • Access to administrative tools is restricted to designated administrators with multi-factor authentication.
  • We follow the principle of least privilege — services we integrate with see only the data they need.

No system is perfectly secure. If we become aware of a security incident affecting your personal data, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

6. Your rights and choices

  • Access & export — you can request a copy of the personal data we hold about your account.
  • Correction — you can ask us to correct information that is inaccurate.
  • Deletion — you can ask us to delete your account and the personal data associated with it. We will do so unless we are legally required to retain it.
  • Withdraw consent — you can stop using the Service at any time. Sign out, and we will retain only the minimum information required for legal or accounting reasons.
  • Local data — generation history, when enabled, lives only on your device. You can clear it from inside the app at any time.
  • Subscription cancellation — manage or cancel your subscription at any time from inside the app or by writing to us.

To exercise any of these rights, email flowwrite@u11.ca from the address tied to your account.

7. Data retention

We retain your account and usage data while your account is active. If you delete your account, we delete or anonymise your personal data within a reasonable period, unless a longer retention is required by law (for example, payment records that must be kept for tax purposes).

8. Children

FlowWrite is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

9. International transfers

FlowWrite operates from Canada and uses service providers that may process data in the United States, the European Union, and elsewhere. By using the Service you understand that your data may be processed outside your country of residence, under safeguards consistent with applicable data-transfer requirements.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top. Material changes will be highlighted with notice inside the app or by email. Continued use of the Service after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact

Questions, requests, or concerns about this policy? Email us at flowwrite@u11.ca. We typically respond within a few business days.
