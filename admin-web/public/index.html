FAQ

Questions, answered

How does the voice dictation work? ▾

Press your dictation key and talk. FlowWrite transcribes your speech, cleans up grammar and filler words, and inserts the polished text at your cursor — in whatever app you're using.

What languages are supported? ▾

Dictation understands 100+ languages and auto-detects which you're speaking. Translation covers 30+ languages, so you can hold a conversation across languages without leaving your chat.

Which apps does it work in? ▾

Any app with a text field — email, browsers, Slack, Notion, messaging apps, your CRM and more. FlowWrite detects where you are and tailors the result, then pastes it straight back in.

Is my data private? ▾

Yes. Your content is only sent when you trigger FlowWrite, it's never used to train AI models, and audio isn't stored. Everything is encrypted in transit. See the Security section above.

What platforms are supported? ▾

macOS (Apple Silicon & Intel) and Windows 64-bit. The macOS build is signed and notarized by Apple, so it opens with no security warnings.
